Why do I love Anime? What is Anime?

Anime is a just entertainment for Adults and kids alike, It teaches the importance of hardwork, determination, respect for one's country and much more, Anime is not just a show but an emotion that brings all of us fans to a understanding and appreciation of what we have surrounding us.

I believe in a world where the entire population will finally realize the worth of anime and not just give it a title of a kid's show.
